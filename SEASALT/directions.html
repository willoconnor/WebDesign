Parking

There is a parking lot at the main entrance of the park and pay boxes are located at the head of the path that leads to Sea Salt. You may also find metered parking on the street within the park. The smaller side lot adjacent to the round-a-bout is for temporary Longfellow House visitors and Sea Salt staff.
