A person I admire (past or present)

Someone I really admire is Vito Mannone. He's the keeper for Minnesota United FC this year Vito is an Italian player who had played in Europe for his whole career up until this year, and yet he has quickly assimilated into life in Minnesota and become a fan favorite. He's a strong leader for our team, and has been named Man of the Match countless times for his consistent work ethic. After almost every win, he walks a slow lap around the entire field clapping his hands for the fans, which I think is really classy and shows how much he cares about this team and its fan base. I was lucky enough to meet Vito and get his autograph at the State Fair this year; he's even taller up close!
